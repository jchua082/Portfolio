BusMeet

Year:

Skills:
2021 React Native, JavaScript, UX Design, Figma, Agile Software Development

Project Scope

To create an integrated application where users can check bus information while interacting with other users, such as messaging and sharing their location and estimated time of arrival (ETA) seamlessly.

Introduction

As users of the SG BusLeh App, our team found it tedious to navigate between the bus app and updating your current location and ETA when meeting up with someone. After conducting surveys and interviews with our target audience, we quickly realised that our surveyees had the same sentiments and would love to include more features to the current app to streamline the current user flow.

Thus, this served as an inspiration for our mobile application, BusMeet, to create an integrated app with all the necessary functions to meet up when travelling via public transport to address our users' needs.

Ideation & Design

From our user research, our team identified 3 new features to solve our users’ pain points:

1. Create meet-up events where users can set up time and location to meet up with other users
2. View users’ ETA based on the location sharing feature
3. Create group chats to communicate with other users without switching apps

Our team then proceeded to come up with new user flows, sitemap, wireframes, assets, avatars, and prototypes utilising Figma. Prototypes are continuously refined after user testing, further streamlining user flows.

App Development

Once our final prototype was finalised, we then proceeded to begin our app development process.

We utilised the Expo framework using React Native for our application development and Amazon Web Services (AWS) Amplify to set up the backend infrastructure of our application.

Enhancements for the app are continuously made after gathering more feedback from our interviewees and weekly consults with our professor.
