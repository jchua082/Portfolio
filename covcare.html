Skills:

Project Scope

To develop a website for a fictional medical company that does PCR testing in Singapore to increase booking sales of PCR packages.

User Research

To understand our target audience better, my team and I created user personas to represent our target audience to analyse their behaviour patterns, goals, skills, attitudes, and background information.

A customer journey map was also created to visualise the user’s journey with the PCR services at all touch points such that we can increase customer retentions and discover loopholes that needs to be improved.

A SWOT Analysis was also conducted to discover the pros and cons of current medical websites.

Designing Process

After conducting user research, we then kickstart the designing process of the website using Figma. After creating a sitemap, we started designing wireframes to experiment with various layouts. We then proceeded to finalise the theme, assets, colour palette, and fonts to be used to best suit our clients’ brand image and achieve the objectives of increasing PCR sales.

After reiterating the refinement process from our target audience's feedback, we finalised our high-fidelity prototypes on Figma.

Copywriting

To help CovCare achieve their goals of increasing booking sales, persuasive copywriting skills was used. Our team brainstormed promotions placed in our promotional banners that will help entice new customers and retain our existing customers. Statistics and social proof are also seen on the website to increase the company’s reputability and professional credibility.

PCR Related Articles can be found in the ‘Articles’ page to educate site visitors and increase SEO, growing traffic to the website.

Web Development Process

We then began to code the website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap that we learned in class. Each member was tasked to code out 3 webpages each before merging them together.

To refine our webpages even further, we gathered feedback from our professor and surveyees. Enhancements to the website were made, such as using animations, responsiveness, and refining our interface designs. A chatbot was also coded using JavaScript which prevents customers from waiting to receive responses, enhancing the user experience.

After reiterating the designing, copywriting, and coding process using Agile Software Development, I am proud to present to you our final product.
