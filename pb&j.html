Project Scope

To develop a website for a fictional medical company that does PCR test in Singapore such that booking sales of PCR packages within the next 3 months by 20%.

User Research

To understand our target audience better, my team and I created user personas to represent our target audience to analyse their behaviour patterns, goals, skills, attitudes, and background information. A customer journey map to visualise the user’s journey with the PCR services at all touch points such that we can increase customer retentions anf discover loopholes that needs to be improved.

Designing Process

After conducting user research, we then kickstart the designing of the website using Figma. After deciding all the various web pages that we would create, we then designed low-fidelity prototypes of the various layouts. We then proceeded to finalise the theme and fonts to be used to best suit our clients’ brand image and achieve the objectives or increasing PCR sales.

After reiterating the refinement process, we finally created our high-fidelity prototypes on Figma.

Copywriting

To help CovCare achieve their goals of increasing booking sales, persuasive copywriting skills were used. Our team brainstormed promotions placed in our promotional banners that will help entice new customers and retain our existing customers. Statistics and social proof are also seen on the website to increase the company’s reputability and professional credibility.
For more details on our copywriting, view our powerpoint pitch here:

Web Development Process

We then began to code the website utilising our HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap skills that we learned in class. We split our workload equally and each member was tasked to code out 3 of the webpages each. To refine our webpages even further, we gathered feedback from our professor and added animations and responsiveness to our site. A chatbot was also included using JavaScript which prevents customers from waiting to receive responses and enhances the user experience. After reiterating the designing, copywriting, and coding process, I am proud to present to you our final product of this project.
