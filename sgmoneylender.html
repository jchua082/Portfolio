Project Scope

Create 3 HTML pages to generate leads for the website of a fictitious money lending company (SG Money Lender) and to build a reputable and approachable brand image.

Creative Direction

The bad reputation of illegal money lenders may paint an unsavoury picture for other legal money lenders in Singapore. Thus, building trust and brand reputation in the companyâ€™s website is vital to entice new customers and retain them.

Therefore, we will leverage this as our main selling point and build a trustworthy brand image in our web design through a modern revamp to generate more leads.

Designing Process

With our creative direction, we first decided on the theme of our websites through the use of colours and fonts to communicate the brandâ€™s trustworthy image effectively. Through multiple discussions, we finalised our primary colour as Royal Blue (#4169E1) as this colour is often associated with trustworthiness and reliability and symbolises empathy and dependability. These are emotions that we would like to evoke on our viewers, making it a perfect primary colour to use in our website.

We chose a sans serif font (Open Sans) to ensure our content is clear and readable, as well as to give a contemporary look to the website. A transitional serif typeface (Libre Baskerville) is also used to create a modern and trustworthy look to the webpages.
