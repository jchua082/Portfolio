Shopee Design Challenge

UX / UI DESIGN


Year:

2022

Skills:

UX Design, Figma

Project Scope

To strengthen ShopeePay Near Me’s positioning as a top-of-mind destination for offline discovery.

Introduction

ShopeePay Near Me provides a one-stop solution to help users discover places to go, and good deals near them. It operates on an online-to-offline (O2O) business model that strives to bridge the gap between online customers and physical stores seamlessly by utilising location-based services to personalise the offerings. Our team was tasked to propose solutions within 3 days to strengthen ShopeePay Near Me's positioning.

User Research

To leverage on the experience and knowledge of existing users, and to gain a better sense of consumer’s preferences, we have conducted a survey to understand the extent of familiarity and perspectives which our target audience holds. At a sample size of 21, the survey sheds novel insights to the negative perceptions and impressions which the target audience holds, deriving the top 3 concerns and improvements to fill.

We also created User Personas and a Customer Journey Map which unveiled pain points in the current interface that prevented customers from having a seamless experience with this feature.

Proposed Solutions

We have designed a mobile application interface, optimising the layout of an iPhone 13 Pro on Figma. Functionality of the site should remain geographically similar to the current platform available in the market. We mainly focused on restructuring the flow of the interface, to ensure intuitiveness and bridge the gaps identified by our surveyees.

Design Mock-Ups

Finally, we designed an interactive mock up of our proposed solution with Figma to better convey our idea.
