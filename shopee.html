Shopee Design Challenge

UX / UI DESIGN

Year:

Skills:

Project Scope

To strengthen ShopeePay Near Me’s positioning as a top-of-mind destination for offline discovery.

Introduction

ShopeePay Near Me provides a one-stop solution to help users discover places to go, and good deals near them. It operates on an online-to-offline (O2O) business model that strives to bridge the gap between online customers and physical stores seamlessly by utilising location-based services to personalise the offerings. Our team was tasked to propose solutions within 3 days to strengthen ShopeePay Near Me's positioning.

User Research

To gain a better sense of consumer’s preferences, we conducted a survey with existing ShopeePay users to understand their extent of familiarity with the ShopeePay Near Me. At a sample size of 21, the survey sheds valuable insights and areas of improvment, such as:

  • Cluttered Layout of Recommended Deals
  • Ease of Navigation
  • Customisation of Recommendations


We also created User Personas and a Customer Journey Map which further unveiled pain points in the current interface that prevented customers from having a seamless user experience.

Proposed Solutions

To tackle the users' pain points, our team proposed innovative solutions to mitigate these problems, such as:

  • Include a live map for better visualisation of nearby deals
  • Include ratings, price range, and reviews for users to make an informed decision before purchasing vouchers/deals
  • Gamification with rewards which incentivises users to continuously use this feature
We designed prototypes on Figma to better visualise the product specifications proposed. We mainly focused on restructuring the flow of the interface and including new features to ensure intuitiveness and bridge the gaps identified by our surveyees.

Design Mock-Ups

Finally, we successfully designed an interactive mock up of our proposed solutions within 3 days on Figma to better convey our ideas.
